Recommended Courses

Quantum Computing Fundamentals

Beginner

Comprehensive introduction to quantum computing concepts, including qubits, gates, circuits, and measurement.

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Format: Video lectures, quizzes, hands-on labs
  • Prerequisites: Basic linear algebra

Quantum Algorithms

Intermediate

Deep dive into quantum algorithms, including Shor's, Grover's, quantum phase estimation, and quantum machine learning.

  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Format: Video lectures, programming assignments
  • Prerequisites: Quantum computing basics

DNA-Based Security Systems

Advanced

Explores DNA computing principles applied to cryptography, self-repairing systems, and quantum-DNA hybrid security.

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Format: Seminars, research papers, projects
  • Prerequisites: Cryptography, basic molecular biology

Quantum Machine Learning

Advanced

Comprehensive course on quantum approaches to machine learning, covering quantum neural networks, SVMs, and clustering.

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Format: Lectures, programming projects
  • Prerequisites: Classical ML, quantum computing
